# Code of Conduct

This project expects direct, respectful, technically grounded collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be clear, constructive, and specific.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and workflow decisions.
- Assume good faith while leaving room for firm technical disagreement.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or hateful conduct.
- Personal attacks, insults, or repeated bad-faith escalation.
- Doxxing, privacy violations, or posting sensitive information.
- Spam or manipulative use of AI-generated content to overwhelm review.

## Scope

This applies to repository spaces such as issues, pull requests, reviews, and any
linked project communication channels used for this project.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, hide, lock, or remove content, and may limit or end
participation for behavior that harms the project or its contributors.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues through the most appropriate available maintainer contact
path. If the issue also involves sensitive security information, follow
`SECURITY.md`.
